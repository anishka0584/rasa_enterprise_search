ROOM TYPES AND AMENITIES

What types of rooms are available?
Our partner hotels offer a variety of room types including Standard Single, Standard Double, Twin, Deluxe Double, Family Rooms, Junior Suites, and Suites. Availability depends on the location and property you choose.

Do the rooms have wifi?
Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is standard across all our partner hotels. High-speed options may be available at a small additional charge in some properties.

Is breakfast included?
Breakfast inclusion depends on the rate you select. Many hotels offer a Bed and Breakfast (B&B) rate. You can ask for breakfast options when you contact the hotel directly after booking.

Do you have accessible rooms for guests with disabilities?
Yes, accessible rooms with wheelchair access, grab rails, and roll-in showers are available at most partner hotels. Please mention your accessibility needs when confirming your reservation with the hotel.

Is there parking available?
Parking availability and charges vary by hotel and location. City-centre hotels may charge for parking, while hotels outside city centres often offer free on-site parking. Please check with the hotel directly.

Is there room service?
Room service availability varies by property. Some of our partner hotels offer 24-hour room service while others may have limited hours or no room service at all. We recommend checking with your specific hotel directly after booking to confirm what food and beverage services are available in your room.

Do rooms have air conditioning?
Most hotels in warmer climates and newer properties include air conditioning. Older properties, particularly in cooler climates like the UK, may have limited AC. This will be noted in the hotel details.

Do the rooms have a view?
Room views vary by property and room type. Options may include city views, garden views, sea views, or courtyard views depending on the hotel and location. If a specific view is important to you, we recommend noting it as a special request when contacting the hotel directly after booking — views cannot be guaranteed but hotels will do their best to accommodate

CHECK-IN AND CHECK-OUT

What is the standard check-in time?
Standard check-in is typically from 2:00 PM. Early check-in may be available on request, subject to room availability, and may incur an additional charge.

What is the standard check-out time?
Standard check-out is usually by 11:00 AM. Late check-out can often be arranged with the hotel directly, sometimes for a fee.

Can I check in early or check out late?
Yes, early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and must be requested directly from the hotel. Some hotels offer complimentary early check-in if rooms are ready.


BOOKING RULES AND LIMITS

How many guests can I book for?
We can accommodate bookings for up to 24 guests per reservation.

How many rooms can I book?
You can book a maximum of 10 rooms per booking.

What is the maximum length of stay?
Bookings are limited to a maximum of 14 nights per reservation. For longer stays, please contact our support team.

Can I book for the same day?
Same-day bookings are subject to hotel availability. We recommend booking as early in the day as possible.


CANCELLATIONS AND CHANGES

Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, cancellations are possible. Cancellation policies vary by hotel and rate type. Many standard rates offer free cancellation up to 24 to 48 hours before check-in. Non-refundable rates cannot be cancelled or amended.

How do I modify my booking?
To change your dates, number of guests, or other details, you can start a new booking conversation or contact our support team directly.

What happens if I need to cancel last minute?
Last-minute cancellations within 24 hours of check-in may incur a charge equal to one night's stay, depending on the hotel's policy.


PAYMENT AND PRICING

How do I pay?
Payment is typically handled directly with the hotel at check-in or through the hotel's online portal. We will provide you with a booking reference once confirmed.

Are there any booking fees?
We do not charge a booking fee. You pay the hotel directly at their standard rates.

Can I get a corporate or group discount?
For group bookings of 5 or more rooms or corporate accounts, please contact our support team for tailored pricing.


CHILDREN AND FAMILY

Are children welcome?
Yes, children are welcome at all our partner hotels. Many hotels offer family rooms or interconnecting rooms. Some hotels allow children under a certain age to stay free when sharing a room with adults. Please check the specific hotel policy.

Do you have cots or cribs available?
Many hotels provide travel cots or cribs on request, often at no extra charge. Please mention this when contacting the hotel to confirm your booking.
